Welcome!

At 3D GEO HIDRO PROIECT we want to offer the best engineering and environmental consulting services in the country.

We built our website because we want to have better communication with our clients. This is a good opportunity for our clients to gather all the information they need about geotechnical projects before signing contracts and starting their projects.

Our company is specialized in geotechinical studies for civil and industrial construction projects prepared in compliance with NP 074/2007, hydrogeological studies, technical reports for water usage, environmental impact assessment studies, environmental assessment studies and environmental technical reports.
